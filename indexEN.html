Medical tourism

All over the world, Germany has an outstanding reputation in many fields. In the medical field in particular, it offers an excellent quality compared to international standards, from preventive healthcare to advice, treatment, and rehabilitation. Germany has reached this high level through close cooperation between medical practitioners, researchers, and medical and pharmaceutical industry professionals throughout the country. This is why every year tens of thousands of foreign patients visit German hospitals and clinics to be treated by highly trained and qualified doctors who use only the most modern methods. In addition to offering many medically necessary therapies, Germany is a popular destination for aesthetic procedures, which, when employed responsibly, can contribute to a heightened sense of well-being. Services

Consultation
Consultationclose

After receiving and, if necessary, translating the diagnosis and other documents, we will contact the appropriate doctors and hospitals and offer our patients only the best options.

Costs
Costsclose

To provide security for the attending physicians and clinics as well as for the patients, we clarify the details in advance, specifying who will cover the costs of the medical procedures - the states in question or the patients themselves.

Visa
Visaclose

After deciding on a clinic and arranging possible appointments in coordination with the hospital, we will contact the relevant authorities and embassies, apply for the visa, and, if necessary, request an extension.

Flights
Flightsclose

In cooperation with travel agencies, we book all flights - if necessary also via MedEvac.

Accommodations
Accommodationsclose

In the patient agreement, we organize accommodation for accompanying persons in hotels or apartments.

Support and translation services
Support and translation servicesclose

Our team consists of highly qualified interpreters, who accompany the patients to the doctor and the hospital and assist them during their stay. We offer interpreters for Arabic, German, English, Spanish, and French.

Transport
Transportclose

We organize all necessary and desired transportation services - including transportation in specially equipped medical vehicles, in the case that this is deemed necessary.

Tourism
Tourismclose

After their treatment, patients have the option of visiting numerous tourist destinations in Germany and neighboring countries, where they can spend a relaxing holiday in a city, a spa, or the countryside, which can help to facilitate a more complete recovery.
